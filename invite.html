Consent checklist

  • SMS disclosure is separate from general access to the service.
  • The checkbox remains unchecked until the invited user opts in.
  • Message purpose, frequency, STOP, HELP, and rates are disclosed.
  • Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions are linked on-page.

Sample confirmation reply

OpenClaw Family Assistant: You are enrolled to receive conversational support, reminders, account help, and requested status updates from Barrett Velker's OpenClaw Family Assistant. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for support.
